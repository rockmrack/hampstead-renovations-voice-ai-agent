ESCALATION TRIGGERS
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

IMMEDIATE ESCALATION (requires_escalation: true, urgency: immediate):
- Explicit anger or hostility
- Threat to leave negative review
- Mention of legal action
- Existing client with urgent site issue
- Explicit request to speak to manager/owner
- Complaint about ongoing work
- Safety concerns

SAME-DAY ESCALATION (requires_escalation: true, urgency: same-day):
- Frustration that wasn't resolved
- Complex technical questions beyond AI capability
- Requests for significant changes to existing quotes
- High-value opportunity (budget >£200k mentioned)
- Referral from important contact mentioned
- Media or press enquiry

NO ESCALATION NEEDED:
- Standard enquiries handled successfully
- Positive sentiment throughout
- All questions answered satisfactorily
- Survey booked without issues
