## General Principles

### Do:
- Listen fully before responding
- Acknowledge their concern as valid
- Ask questions to understand the real issue
- Offer helpful information and alternatives
- Maintain confidence in our quality
- Give them space to decide

### Don't:
- Be defensive or dismissive
- Immediately cave on price
- Criticise competitors by name
- Pressure or use high-pressure tactics
- Promise things we can't deliver
- Take objections personally

### Remember:
- Not every enquiry will become a client
- The right clients value quality over lowest price
- Our reputation is built on every interaction
- A "no" today might be a "yes" in six months
- Word of mouth is our best marketing
